Question 4 (Extra): Is it possible to find a correlation between mental health and gun violence?


To answer this question, we realized that it required data about mental health care in every state and it required data about gun violence per capita. Per state, for every incident, we would divide it by the population of that state in that particular month and sum it up. Mental Health America provided us with a mental health care ranking of states. Unfortunately, the data was ranked from ‘best state to worst state’ instead of an actual score, since the latter would probably be more precise.
